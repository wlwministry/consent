General

I confirm I am the person named on this form, and I seek prayer ministry of my own free will.

This is a Christian ministry that is based on Biblical principles and involves recipients in a time of prayer and listening to God the Father, in the name of Jesus and empowered by the Holy Spirit. Through prayer, biblical principles, and active listening, the approach seeks to identify root causes of ongoing struggles and facilitate encounters with God's healing presence.

Appointments are not provided for people not present and consenting.

Volunteer prayer ministers:
Prayer ministry appointments are run by a minimum of two trained volunteer prayer ministers with police clearances. I may be asked if an additional WLWM team member can attend my appointment for the purpose of their development in prayer ministry. I have the right to decline.

Appointment based:
WLWM provides prayer ministry within the context of appointments, and is not set up to provide ongoing support in between appointments.

Appointments do not replace:

  • Professional treatment or services such as relationship counselling, addiction counselling, medical treatment, psychological therapy, financial advice or career counselling.
  • Pastoral care from involvement with a local church.

WLWM reserves the right to:

  • Decline to provide appointments or continue to provide them where assessed that a person requires supports outside of their scope. This decision will not be made on the basis of attributes such as age, disability, race, sex, sexual orientation or religious belief; but rather WLWM's capacity to provide the requested prayer ministry in a way that is timely, safe, sustainable and Biblical.
  • Redirect towards more appropriate avenues of support.
Confidentiality & Privacy

I consent to my information being used as per WLWM's privacy policy. In particular:

  • I understand that information provided by me will be considered private and confidential; and will not be sold to or shared with any third party without my consent or request.
  • I may be asked if I wish to share my experience or testimony with WLWM for the purpose of encouragement of WLWM team members or people considering prayer ministry. This is entirely optional. I can define how my testimony is shared (e.g. anonymous, wording, with whom — e.g. WLWM team members only).
  • I have the right to later withdraw or change any consent to share information.

Exceptions to confidentiality include:

  • Concerns for the safety of myself or others.
  • Requirement by law (such as reporting of child abuse).
  • Information ordered or otherwise reasonably required in legal proceedings.
  • At times we may seek your consent to discuss how best to support you, e.g. with another church staff member who also observes confidentiality agreement.

Records of appointments are kept for legal requirements. They do not contain personal details unless required for safeguarding, are strictly confidential and can be accessed by myself in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles.

Experiences, Outcomes & Liability

I understand and acknowledge that:

  • Prayer ministry may involve discussing past and current sensitive personal matters.
  • People can experience a variety of responses involving their emotions, mind, body or spirit, including distress during or after prayer ministry. This can include exacerbation of existing physical or mental health conditions.
  • Prayer ministry outcomes vary between individuals and cannot be predicted or guaranteed.
  • I am responsible for my own decisions during and after prayer ministry appointments. For example, if I change or cease any treatment or therapy for health conditions without first consulting the relevant health professional, I do so at my own risk.
  • WLWM volunteers will exercise reasonable care in providing prayer ministry, but are not liable for outcomes that result from inherent risks of discussing personal matters that I have been informed about.
  • WLWM remains responsible for ensuring its team members are properly trained, exercise due care, and act within appropriate boundaries and ethical standards.
  • If I have concerns, I am encouraged to contact WLWM's coordinator or WLWM leadership.
I Agree That
Voluntary participation I have considered whether prayer ministry is right for me at this time. I can decline to discuss any topic or stop the appointment at any time.
Acknowledgment of inherent risks I understand that discussing sensitive personal matters may cause emotional distress, and that outcomes from prayer ministry cannot be guaranteed. I voluntarily accept these inherent risks.
My responsibility for my own actions If a third party makes a claim against WLWM arising from my own misconduct or actions (not related to how WLWM volunteers conducted the ministry), I will be responsible for associated costs.
Safety To help WLWM provide appropriate support and maintain a safe environment for everyone, I agree to disclose relevant information including:
  • Recent infectious symptoms (e.g. cold/flu in the last 7 days)
  • Severe allergies that may require emergency response
  • Current safety concerns (e.g. domestic violence, substance use with risk of withdrawal)
  • Recent suicidal thoughts or severe distress
